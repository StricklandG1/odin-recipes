Steak Quesadilla Recipe


Description:

Totally awesome Steak Quesadilla recipe



Ingredients:

  • 1x lb sirloin steak, thinly sliced
  • 2x small onions, sliced
  • 2x medium green bell peppers, sliced
  • 1x cup BBQ sauce
  • 8x 10in. flour tortillas
  • 2x cups shredded cheddar cheese


Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit (220 degrees Celcius)
  2. Cook and stir steak in a skillet over medium heat until browned, 5 to 7 minutes
  3. Add onions and bell peppers, cook and stir until softened, 5 to 10 minutes
  4. Pour BBQ sauce over steak mixture and simmer until sauce is slightly reduced, 10 minutes
  5. Lay 4 tortillas on a baking sheet, top each with steak mixture, Cheddar cheese, and remaining tortillas
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes. Flip quesadillas and cook until cheese is melted
