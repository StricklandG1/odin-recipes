Cheesecake Recipe


Description:

Totally awesome cheesecake recipe



Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups Honey Maid Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • 1/3 cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 3x tablespoons sugar
  • 4x 8oz packages Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened
  • 1x cup sugar
  • 1x tsp vanilla
  • 4x eggs


Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit (165 degrees Celcius)
  2. Mix crumbs, butter, and 3 tbsp sugar until combined. Press firmly onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan
  3. Beat cream cheese, 1 cup sugar, and vanila in a mixing bowl until smooth
  4. add eggs, one at a time, mixing on low speed until just blended. Pour over crust
  5. Bake in preheated oven until center is almost set, 45-55 minutes
  6. Loosen cake from pan rim, cool before removing rim
  7. Refrigerate for 4 hours before serving
