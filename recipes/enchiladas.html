Enchilada Recipe


Description:

Totally awesome enchilada recipe



Ingredients:

  • 1x 15oz can tomato sauce
  • 1x 6oz can tomato paste
  • 2 tsp Creaole-style seasoning
  • 1x 12oz package corn tortillas
  • 1x 8oz package cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1x onion, diced
  • 1x 6oz can sliced ripe olives
  • 1x 6oz can sliced mushrooms


Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celcius)
  2. In a medium bowl, combine tomato sauce, tomato paste, and Creole-style seasoning
  3. Warm tortillas in microwave or in oven, dip in tomato sauce mixture, and place in casserole dish
  4. Fill each tortilla with cheese, onion, olives, and mushrooms. Roll
  5. Repeat step 4 until dish is full. Sprinkle cheese on top
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted
